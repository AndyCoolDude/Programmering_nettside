Most people are being monitored and controlled by what they see on social media
Social media like facebook or tiktok are designed for us to spend as much time as possible on the websites.
I am personally interested in gaming, and this means that i will see something completely different than someone who is interested in football.
Since we have clicked on different reccomended videos and pictures, we see different things because the algorithm knows what we want. When we see what we want to see, we can sometimes block out everything we disagree with. This can make us believe certain things.
If i only see articles about the world being flat and covid-19 being a hoax, it will be hard for me to believe otherwise.
Big tech companies can sell our information and use it to learn how to best profit from us.
